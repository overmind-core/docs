# Fine-tuning (Beta)

Fine-tuning in Overmind is currently in beta. Learn benefits and how to get early access.

Overmind fine-tuning trains private models on your production traces and evaluation workflows. It is **currently in beta** and not available for self-serve use.

## Availability

[Section titled “Availability”](https://docs.overmindlab.ai/#availability)

Contact [support@overmindlab.ai](mailto:support@overmindlab.ai) to discuss access.

## Recommended path

[Section titled “Recommended path”](https://docs.overmindlab.ai/#recommended-path)

1. Enable tracing in production.
2. Optimize critical flows with the Overmind Optimizer.
3. Contact us to discuss fine-tuning beta access.
