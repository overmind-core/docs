Overmind fine-tuning trains private models directly on your production traces and evaluation workflows. This gives your agents a model that reflects your real workloads — not generic benchmarks. Fine-tuning is **currently in beta** and is not available for self-serve use.

## Availability

<Warning>
  Fine-tuning is in private beta and requires manual access approval. Contact
  [support@overmindlab.ai](mailto:support@overmindlab.ai) to discuss access for
  your team.
</Warning>

## What fine-tuning offers

Fine-tuning your agents with Overmind gives you:

- **Private model customization** — models trained exclusively on your data, never shared across tenants
- **Production-grounded training** — training corpus is built from your real production traces, not synthetic or generic data
- **Eval workflow integration** — fine-tuning plugs directly into your existing Overmind eval setup, so you can measure improvement without rebuilding your pipeline

## Recommended path

Follow these steps to set yourself up for a successful fine-tuning engagement.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable tracing in production">
    Instrument your agent to emit traces before requesting fine-tuning access.
    Fine-tuning quality depends directly on the volume and quality of captured
    traces. See the [Tracing Overview](/docs/tracing/overview) to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize critical flows with the Overmind Optimizer">
    Run the Optimizer on your most important agent workflows to generate
    high-quality iteration data. This data forms part of the training corpus for
    fine-tuning. See [How the Optimizer Works](/docs/optimizer/how-it-works) for
    details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact us to discuss fine-tuning beta access">
    Once you have tracing enabled and at least one optimization job complete,
    reach out to [support@overmindlab.ai](mailto:support@overmindlab.ai). Include
    a brief description of your use case and the agent flows you want to
    fine-tune.
  </Step>
</Steps>
